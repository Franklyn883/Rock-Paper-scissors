About
Rock, Paper and Scissors

Rock Paper Scissors is a classic hand game played between two people. It's a game of chance that's easy to learn and fun to play. In this digital version of the game, you'll play against the computer. The winner will be determined based on the weapon each player chooses.

How to Play

To play the game, simply click on one of the three choices - rock, paper, or scissors - to select your weapon. The computer will also randomly select its weapon, and the winner will be determined based on the following rules:

  • Rock beats scissors
  • Scissors beat paper
  • Paper beats rock
The game is won by the first player to score 5 points. At the end of each round, a message will be displayed to indicate the winner. Good luck and have fun!
